Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2025

Tyrone Treadwell Coaching respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or apply for coaching services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, age, fitness goals, and any other details you submit via forms.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data such as browser type, time spent on the site, and pages visited (via tools like Google Analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To review coaching applications and communicate with you.
  • To improve our website and user experience.
  • To send updates, promotions, or service announcements (you can opt out at any time).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except:

  • With service providers like Google Forms for data collection.
  • When required by law.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You can request to:

  • Access or correct your data.
  • Withdraw consent or request deletion of your information.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, email us at: TheTyroneTreadwell@gmail.com
